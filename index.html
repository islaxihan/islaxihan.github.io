Robotics

My research interests lie in the middle of multi-agent systems, human-robot collaboration, and stigmergic construction. Specifically, I'm curious to explore how a heterogeneous team consisting of multiple humans and robots can design and build creative and high-performing structures by making local decisions on-the-fly.
